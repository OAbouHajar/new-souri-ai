# **Syrian Transitional Government — Key Facts &** **Ministers (2025)**

**President:** Ahmed alISharaa (since 29 January 2025)


The Syrian transitional government is led by President Ahmed alISharaa. There is no Prime
Minister in the transitional setup. The government is composed of 22I23 ministries covering major
functions. Some ministries have been newly established, such as Emergency & Disaster
Management.
